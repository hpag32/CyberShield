🛡 Cyber Security Tips

🔐 Use Strong Passwords

Create passwords with at least 8 characters, including uppercase letters, lowercase letters, numbers, and special symbols.

📱 Enable Multi-Factor Authentication

Add an extra layer of security by enabling MFA wherever possible.

⚠️ Beware of Phishing

Never click suspicious links or open attachments from unknown senders.

🔄 Keep Software Updated

Regularly update your operating system, browser, and antivirus software.

🌐 Browse Secure Websites

Always check for HTTPS before entering personal or financial information.

💾 Back Up Important Data

Keep backups of important files using cloud storage or an external drive.
